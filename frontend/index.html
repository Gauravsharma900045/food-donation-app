Simple Process

How FoodShare Works

From surplus to service in 4 easy steps.

1
📸
Post Your Food
Donors list surplus food with photos, quantity, location & pickup time in minutes.
2
🔍
Browse & Discover
Receivers browse real-time listings sorted by nearest distance to their location.
3
🔔
Request & Get Notified
One-click request sends an instant notification to the donor. No calls needed.
4
🍽
Pickup & Feed
Receiver collects the food at the listed location. Donor marks it as completed.
